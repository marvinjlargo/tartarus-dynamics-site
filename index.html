About Tartarus Dynamics

Tartarus Dynamics LLC is a partnership founded by Rubén Alvarenga, Marvin Largo, and Reece Meakings. Based in the United States, we specialize in creating cutting-edge agricultural technology solutions that drive efficiency and innovation in the farming sector.

Our mission is to deliver modern, reliable, and user-friendly products that help farmers and ranchers manage their livestock with ease, accuracy, and peace of mind.

SpacePar: Smart Livestock Ear Tag

SpacePar is a revolutionary ear tag system designed for livestock. It offers:

  • Localization function (initially based on farm antennas)
  • Sound alert in case the tag is lost in the field
  • GPS integration planned for future versions
  • One-year battery life, supported by mini solar cells
  • Animal identification with data accessible through scanning at handling facilities
  • Associated software with offline and online capabilities:
    • A desktop application for offline data management
    • A web-based system with a cloud-synced database
