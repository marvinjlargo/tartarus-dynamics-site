About Tartarus Dynamics

Tartarus Dynamics LLC is a visionary partnership founded by Rubén Alvarenga, Marvin Largo, and Reece Meakings. Based in the United States, we innovate cutting-edge agricultural technology solutions that drive efficiency and transform the farming sector.

Our mission is to deliver modern, reliable, and user-friendly products that empower farmers and ranchers to manage their livestock with unmatched ease and precision.

SpacePar: Smart Livestock Ear Tag

SpacePar is engineered to revolutionize livestock management by offering:

  • Precise localization using farm antennas
  • Audible alerts if a tag goes missing
  • Future-ready GPS integration
  • One-year battery life enhanced by mini solar cells
  • Quick animal identification via scanning at handling facilities
  • Integrated software solutions:
    • Robust offline desktop application
    • Dynamic web-based system with seamless cloud syncing
